About

Frankfurt, a central German city on the river Main, is a major financial hub that's home to the European Central Bank. The city is the heart of the larger Rhine-Main metropolitan region, which has a population of more than 5.6 million and is Germany's second-largest metropolitan region after the Rhine-Ruhr region.
